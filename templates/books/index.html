About

PLM Library Search is Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila’s new unified discovery system that streamlines your search for the university's research papers.


PLM Library Search includes:

  • Filipiniana Section
  • Health & Sciences Library
  • Graduate School Library


PLM Library Search does not include everything the library offers. (ex: Research papers dated 2016 or earlier, and other collections.)

Service Hours

Onsite library services are available from Monday to Friday at 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. during regular school days. This excludes Saturdays, Sundays, holidays and suspensions.


Authorized Library Users:

  • Library privileges are granted only to all bona fide PLM students, faculty members and employees
  • A maximum of one (1) hour per day per library user is allowed
  • Limited library users are allowed at a time in each Section or Unit Library.
